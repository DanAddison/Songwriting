## Low Light
just because  
my pride  
has left  
me  
don’t doubt that I’m  
a lion  
in Daniel’s  
skin  

i move in low light

it’s such a thrill  
rolling  
in grass  
beyond the village  
from here the stars  
spell out  
your human  
stories  

i move in low light  

you’re not my friends
