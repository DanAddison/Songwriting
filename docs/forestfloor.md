## Begin Again

When you wake you will forget you had a name  
a name that tugs and drags as the clothes on your back  
beneath the swell  

As your eyes adjust an unfamiliar world will open up  
and you are lost  
you are only where you fell  

Stirring still, indistinct and so complete  
a fever dream against your skin  
i am so real  

A shock of white on a wing  
blown speaker cones  
a taste on your tongue now  
a quickening  

When you wake you will forget you had a name  
When you wake you will begin again  

Of course  
No one warns you

## Long Way Home

I took the long way home to you my love  
I don't know what I was thinking of  
Something nihilistic and forlorn  
I was wrong, I was wrong  

You took the long way home to me yourself  
I don't know what thoughts were in your head  
Something I have never understood  
But you'd walked and you'd walked enough  
It was long, it was long it was  

High ridges plunging into pines  
Brackish marsh, a brook, a stand of limes  
Dappled sunlight softening your form  

Samphire and danish scurvy grass  
Gilded lay-bys of iridescent glass  
Tired edgelands, cable work and cranes  
I was lost, I was lost I was  
Beneath the spires and across the moors
